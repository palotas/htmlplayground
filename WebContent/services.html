Test Automation Assessments

A test automation assessment will provide you with an indepth view on the current state of your automation efforts and areas for improvement. You will receive step by step instructions on how to build on your current capabilities and to reach your objectives.
These engagements typically last from 2-5 days onsite at your company.

The assessment focuses on:
  • General test automation approach
  • Test environment setup and configuration
  • Proper use of modularization (i.e. page objects)
  • Code reviews
  • Scaling and virtualization
  • Selenium GRID configuration
  • Analysis of unstable tests
