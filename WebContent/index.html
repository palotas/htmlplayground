Services & Solutions

GRIDFUSION specializes in test consulting and test automation with open source toolsets. We cover the entire value chain from the first assessment of your organisation to assisting you with the actual implementation of the solution.

An engagement with GRIDFUSION allows you to tap into expert knowledge around Selenium and how to best use it within your organization.

Training

We offer training courses for individuals, teams or whole organizations. The courses are hands on and you will write automated tests yourself. We also offer highly customized courses, which fit to your organization's specific needs.

Our philosophy

Test automation is an increasingly important part of the software development and test lifecycle in today’s software industry. Reliable test automation is one of the key enablers for agile development, continuous delivery and a generally higher software quality.

GRIDFUSION is not a tool vendor and we consider our engagement with you a success once you have the capabilities and knowledge to independently reach your test automation objectives.
