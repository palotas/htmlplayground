Why, what, when, who and how should you automate your tests? While these seem like easy questions we strongly recommend to spend some time on each of these questions before you start out. Below is our point of view:

  • WHY?

    • Saving time and to be able to reinvest that time into higher level and higher value test activities shuold be your primary goal. Regression testing is usually a repetitive but very time consuming task, especially in agile environments where you will see a regression test case explosion from iteration to iteration.

    • Of course as early as possible and ideally before the iteration is over. Even better if you have a TDD approach where the tests are written before the business logic. However in our opinion it is perfectly ok to automate after the release has gone out. In many cases the product is not stable during the iterations and therefore trying to keep up with automation can sometimes be challenging.
