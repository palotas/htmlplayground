Our philosophy

GRIDFUSION is not a tool vendor. The Selenium tool family is open source and therefore free to use. An engagement with GRIDFUSION allows you to tap into expert knowledge around Selenium and how to best use it for your organization. We consider ourselves successful when we have passed on enough knowledge so you don't need us any longer.
