Our philosophy

We specialize in consulting and training for the Selenium tool family. An engagement with GRIDFUSION allows you to tap into expert knowledge around Selenium and how to best use it within your organization. Selenium/Webdriver is quickly becoming the standard tool in many companies around the globe due to its flexibility, cost effectiveness and wide support for all major browsers.

GRIDFUSION is not a tool vendor and we consider ourselves successful when we have passed on enough knowledge so you don't need us any longer.
