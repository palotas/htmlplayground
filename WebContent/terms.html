30 days upon receipt of invoice.

Registration
Registration is made electronically online by the participant or the department within his company handling the registration. Registration is only finalized when the delegate’s names are provided plus full invoicing details (Contact name, company name, address).

Notice by the Attendee
Cancellation of registration is done electronically via Email to info@gridfusion.net. Cancellation refunds are as follows:

>20 calendar days before Event will receive a 75% refund.

Participants shall have no legal claim to the reimbursement of travel and/or accommodation expenses or to compensation for loss of working hours.
