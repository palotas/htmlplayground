While GridFusion's core business is test automation, a few words on manual testing:

Manual testing is and (in our opinion) will always be one of the most important pillars on software testing. Test automation only works in conjunction with manual testing. In fact most bugs are actually found during manual testing and not through automation. Automation helps to reduce the repetitive test effort which then can be reinvested into i.e. exploratory types of testing.

Do not measure the ROI of automation by how many bugs you find through it!!!
